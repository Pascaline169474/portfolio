BEAUTY SHELL

....more than beauty

Beauty is what most people, if not all people crave for, most especially the women; they are ready to go the extra mile just to look beautiful. Beauty gives to many a sense of confidence and pride. The Beauty business is a very lucrative one and it has a lot of competition owning to the fact that fashion is an ever changing concept.  Beauty Shell is a one stop unisex full-service beauty salon committed towards giving clients (men, women and children) the best experience there is, thereby consistently providing high customer satisfaction by rendering excellent service, quality products, and a well-furnished and enjoyable atmosphere at an acceptable price/value relationship. The people of today have numerous demand including jobs, child care, household management, and a myriad of other activities. They need fast and convenient services. If one can accommodate the needs of today’s modern people, one will be rewarded with significant sales.  We will also strive to maintain a very friendly and creative work environment, which respects diversity, ideas, and hard work. Beauty Shell will commence operations with a wide range of beauty services and products. We will provide excellent hair, barbing, nail, make up and skin care services, along with quality line of beauty products. What will set Beauty Shell apart from the competition is not only our commitment to providing all of these services in one convenient location, but also for our special features in children’s beauty needs and the way we utilize technology in our service delivery.
